INTERNET ACCESS
Here at Lansmash we only have limited internet capacity shared between alot of people. The internet is literally a bunch of 4G internet connections mashed together (we're not even kidding). Not only are these connections limited, they're mighty expensive too. We ask that you try not to download anything large from the internet, or stream Youtube/Twitch/etc. while here. This kind of thing clogs up the limited connection for those who need it for gaming and LAN-related access.


Some rules:
  • Please close any bittorent, usenet, random obscure Korean MMO launchers, or other P2P software you may have running.
  • The usual "no funny business" lecture - if you wouldn't do it in front of a cop, a priest and your grandparents - don't do it
  • Don't try and circument any filtering - it won't work, you'll be caught and you'll be banned / removed


Better places to get that thing you need:
  • Before you go slurping down software and drivers from the internet - Check our download repository for some popular files, such as the graphics drivers and installers for some frequently used software.
  • Check on LANDC (see above) - somebody may be sharing that thing you need.
  • If that fails, ask around. You're in a huge room full of geeks and computers - somebody is going to have that thing you need.
  • These restrictions do not apply to steam games, which are provided from the steam cache server.
